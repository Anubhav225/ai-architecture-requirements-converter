BUSINESS REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (BRD)
Project: MediConnect - Integrated Telehealth & Patient Management Platform
Version: 3.0
Classification: CONFIDENTIAL

========================================================
1. BUSINESS CONTEXT & OBJECTIVES
========================================================

1.1 Business Problem
Healthcare providers face fragmented systems for patient management, scheduling,
telehealth, billing, and clinical documentation. MediConnect unifies these into
a single HIPAA-compliant platform serving hospitals, clinics, and independent
practitioners.

1.2 Business Objectives
- Reduce administrative overhead by 40% for healthcare providers
- Increase patient engagement and appointment adherence by 50%
- Enable telehealth visits for 100% of eligible consultations
- Serve 10,000+ healthcare providers and 5M+ patients

1.3 Regulatory Compliance (MANDATORY)
- HIPAA and HITECH Act compliance
- HL7 FHIR R4 standard for health data exchange
- DICOM for medical imaging
- State-specific telehealth regulations across all US states

========================================================
2. STAKEHOLDER REQUIREMENTS
========================================================

2.1 Healthcare Providers

BR-001: Clinical Documentation
- SOAP note templates with customizable fields by specialty
- AI-assisted clinical note generation from visit transcripts
- Structured data capture: vitals, medications, diagnoses (ICD-10), procedures (CPT)
- E-prescribing with DEA compliance for controlled substances
- Lab order integration with major lab networks
- Clinical decision support alerts for drug interactions and dosing

BR-002: Telehealth Consultations
- HIPAA-compliant video platform with no third-party data sharing
- Virtual waiting room with estimated wait time
- Screen share for showing imaging, documents, education content
- Interpreter services integration for 240+ languages
- Session recording with patient consent, retained per HIPAA requirements
- Low-bandwidth mode with audio-only fallback

BR-003: Schedule Management
- Provider calendar with multi-location support
- Appointment types with duration, resource, and prep requirements
- Real-time schedule sync across all care team members
- Automated conflict detection

2.2 Patients

BR-004: Patient Portal
- Secure login with multi-factor authentication
- View and download health records in HL7 FHIR format
- Lab results with provider annotations and normal range indicators
- Medication list with refill requests

BR-005: Appointment Management
- Online scheduling with real-time slot availability
- Insurance eligibility verification at booking
- Appointment reminders via email, SMS, and push notification
- Smart wait list with automatic backfill on cancellation

BR-006: Secure Messaging
- HIPAA-compliant asynchronous messaging with care team
- Message routing rules based on urgency
- After-hours message routing to on-call provider
- Message retention of 7 years minimum

2.3 Administrative Staff

BR-007: Revenue Cycle Management
- Insurance verification before every appointment
- Prior authorization workflow tracking
- Claims submission, both electronic and paper
- Denial management with root cause analysis and appeal workflow
- Revenue analytics: payer mix, denial rates, days in AR

BR-008: Front Desk Operations
- Patient check-in via kiosk, mobile, or tablet
- Digital intake forms replacing paper processes
- Real-time eligibility check at check-in
- Patient flow tracking through visit stages

2.4 Hospital Administration

BR-009: Analytics & Reporting
- Executive dashboard: patient volume, revenue, quality metrics
- Population health management and care gap identification
- Provider productivity reports
- Custom report builder with scheduled distribution

BR-010: Interoperability & Data Exchange
- Patient data exchange with other EHRs via FHIR APIs
- Health Information Exchange participation
- Prescription Drug Monitoring Program integration

========================================================
3. NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
========================================================

PERFORMANCE:
- Page load time under 2 seconds for 95th percentile
- Video call latency under 150ms end-to-end
- Concurrent users: 50,000 providers plus 500,000 patients simultaneously

AVAILABILITY:
- 99.99% uptime
- Geographic redundancy across multiple regions
- Automatic failover with RTO under 1 minute

SECURITY & COMPLIANCE (NON-NEGOTIABLE):
- HIPAA Business Associate Agreement with all sub-processors
- End-to-end encryption for all Protected Health Information
- AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
- Audit log of every PHI access: who, what, when, from where
- Multi-factor authentication mandatory for all provider accounts
- Annual third-party HIPAA risk assessment

SCALABILITY:
- Support 100,000 concurrent telehealth sessions
- Store 10 petabytes of medical imaging data
- Process 1 million lab results per day

USABILITY:
- ADA WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility compliance
- Mobile-first design for patient portal
- Maximum 3 clicks to complete any common task

INTEGRATION STANDARDS:
- HL7 FHIR R4 for health data APIs
- DICOM for imaging
- EDI X12 for insurance transactions

========================================================
4. CONSTRAINTS
========================================================

- All PHI must remain within US borders unless patient consents otherwise
- Must not use PHI for AI training without explicit patient consent
- Integration with Epic required for hospital deployments
- HIPAA mandates breach notification within 60 days
